Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026 · Rackler Tech Ventures LLC

This Privacy Policy explains how Rackler Tech Ventures LLC ("EdgeDesk," "we," "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the EdgeDesk website and services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address and authentication credentials when you register.
  • Subscription and billing information — if you subscribe, your payment details and billing records are collected and processed by our third-party payment processor. We do not store full payment-card numbers on our own systems.
  • Consent records — a record that you affirmed you are of legal age, that use is lawful where you are, and that you accepted our Terms, including a timestamp and version, retained as required by law.
  • Content you enter — information you choose to save in features such as the bet ledger, stored to provide that feature to you.
  • Usage and device data — technical and interaction data such as pages viewed, features used, approximate location derived from IP, browser and device type, and log data, used to operate, secure, and improve the Service.

2. How we use information

  • To provide, operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Service and your account.
  • To process subscriptions, billing, and renewals, and to send required subscription and renewal notices.
  • To communicate with you about your account, security, and the Service.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not "share" it for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are defined under California law.

3. Service providers we share with

We share information with vendors that process it on our behalf, under contractual and security obligations, only to provide the Service:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and application-data hosting.
  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing, subscription billing, renewal management, and the billing portal. Your card details are entered directly with Stripe and are never stored on our systems.
  • GitHub, Inc. (GitHub Pages) — static hosting and delivery of the website and application files. GitHub receives your IP address and request data as part of serving the site.
  • Google LLC (Google Analytics) — website and product analytics, to understand which features are used and to improve the Service. Google Analytics sets cookies and collects usage and device data, including a randomly generated identifier, pages viewed, referring page, browser and device type, and approximate location derived from your IP address. We do not send Google your email address, your account identifier, or the contents of your bet ledger, and we do not use Google Analytics data for advertising. You can opt out of Google Analytics on every site you visit using Google's browser add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Google LLC (Google Fonts) — web fonts served on our pages; Google receives your IP address when a font is requested.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to respond to lawful requests, to protect our rights, safety, or property, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Retention

We keep account, consent, and usage information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations (including auto-renewal consent recordkeeping), resolve disputes, and enforce agreements, after which we delete or de-identify it.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your credentials confidential.

6. Your rights and choices

  • You may access, correct, or delete your account information, or request a copy of it, by contacting us.
  • California residents (CCPA/CPRA): you have the right to know, access, correct, and delete personal information, and to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" — noting that we do neither. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights. You may designate an authorized agent to act on your behalf.
  • Other U.S. state privacy laws: where applicable (e.g., Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas), you may have comparable rights of access, correction, deletion, portability, and opt-out; contact us to exercise them.
  • EU/UK residents (if applicable): where GDPR or UK GDPR applies, you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Our legal bases include performance of our contract with you, our legitimate interests in operating and securing the Service, and compliance with law.

7. Cookies and local storage

The Service uses cookies, browser local storage, and similar technologies for two purposes:

  • Strictly necessary — to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and store content you enter locally (such as logged bets). The Service will not work without these.
  • Analytics — Google Analytics cookies, which record how the Service is used so we can improve it. These are not used for advertising and are not shared with advertisers.

You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling the strictly necessary ones will impair functionality. To opt out of Google Analytics specifically, without affecting anything else, install Google's opt-out browser add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

8. Children

The Service is intended only for adults 21+ and is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable). If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International users

We operate in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., you understand your information will be processed in the U.S., which may have different data-protection laws than your jurisdiction.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be posted here with a new "last updated" date and, where required, communicated to you.

11. Contact

Rackler Tech Ventures LLC — for any privacy request, including access, correction, deletion, or a copy of your data, contact support@edgedesksports.com from the email address associated with your account so we can verify the request.
