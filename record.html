Method check

Does de-vigging even recover a probability?

Raised by a stranger on r/algobetting, and correct: a posted price is not probability plus vig. Part of it is where the book expects money to land. Stripping the overround to 100% removes the margin and leaves the shading. If de-vigging recovered truth, every book's de-vigged number would agree with Pinnacle's on the same selection. This measures whether they do.
